Opportunity

Weather volatility is now a financial risk
The Problem
01

Climate change makes weather patterns less predictable and more volatile.

02

F&B manufacturers, food retailers and crop traders face direct exposure — unpredictable weather disrupts crop yields, supply chains and drives crop price volatility.

03

These players lack adequate systems to anticipate, model, and hedge crop supply and price risks.

Our Solution We build an AI-native platform that enables F&B manufacturers, food retailers, and agricultural crop traders to anticipate, model, and hedge crop supply and price risks — turning weather volatility from an uncontrollable threat into a manageable risk.

Moat
www.nala.earth
We are building moat across different dimensions
Proprietary Data Intelligence
We are building proprietary models and data layers that grow over time.
  • Weather risk models
  • Yield & price risks data
  • Data base of risk reports (publicly available or shared with users)
  • Judgment data layer of every decision that has been made to train models
Network Effect
Every new user makes the platform smarter. The more users, the stronger our data & intelligence engine becomes, creating a compounding advantage that is hard to replicate.
  • The more data gather through the platform, the more powerful the intelligence
  • The more decisions tracked through the platform, the better the judgement
  • The more users share reports on risks & contract prices, the more powerful the platform becomes for every participant — turning individual insights into collective intelligence
Stickiness and Workflow
We are the system of records and will be integrated into the workflow
  • System of recors for all risk, reports, sourcing regions and past decisions
  • Judgement layer will boost productivity of users and will be highly integrated in their workflow
Distribution
Our hedging solution sits on top of the crop risk intelligence and has huge upselling potential in selling insurance products
